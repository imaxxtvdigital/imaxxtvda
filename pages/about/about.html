IMaxx TV – India's First Hybrid OTT Platform


Who We Are

IMaxx TV Digital Private Limited, popularly known as "IMaxx TV", is India's first hybrid OTT platform, offering a seamless blend of AVOD (Free with Ads), SVOD (Subscription-Based), and TVOD (Pay-Per-View), models. Launched in September 2023 in Mumbai, we are on a mission to deliver high-quality, pocket-friendly entertainment for audiences of all ages, across the globe.


For Viewers:

A vast library of movies, web series, short films, and shows from around the world—stream anytime, anywhere. Watch Free (AVOD): Instant access with ads. Premium Subscriptions (SVOD): Ad-free, unlimited entertainment. Pay-Per-View (TVOD): Exclusive content at your fingertips. For Creators: A powerful platform to upload, monetize, and distribute films, web series, and shows via revenue-sharing or outright sales.


Why Choose IMaxx TV?

Multi-Language Support: Content in various languages with regional & English subtitles. Cross-Device Compatibility: Stream or download on iOS, Android, and Windows 10. Global Reach, Local Touch: Bringing world-class entertainment at affordable pricing.


Our Vision

To democratize entertainment by making cinematic experiences accessible to everyone, at prices that don't break the bank. Whether you're a creator looking for a global stage or a viewer craving fresh content, IMaxx TV brings the world's stories to your screen.
